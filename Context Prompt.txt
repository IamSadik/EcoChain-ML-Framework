## PROJECT OVERVIEW

**Title:** EcoChain-ML: Blockchain-Verified Carbon-Aware Edge ML Inference Through Renewable Energy Prediction

**Core Thesis:** Model compression alone is insufficient for sustainable ML inference. While INT8 quantization achieves 33.69% energy savings, it inadvertently routes tasks to fast grid-powered nodes, causing renewable utilization to DROP from 30.47% to 29.55%. Renewable-aware scheduling is essential to maximize carbon reduction.

**Domain:** Edge ML Inference on heterogeneous devices (Raspberry Pi 4, Intel NUC, Jetson Nano, AMD Ryzen)

---

## FIVE INTEGRATED COMPONENTS

1. **XGBoost Renewable Predictor** - Forecasts solar/wind availability 1-hour ahead
   - Test R² = 0.894, RMSE = 11.41W
   - 5.17% better RMSE than persistence baseline
   - Top features: renewable_lag_1h (35.1%), ALLSKY_SFC_SW_DWN (14.7%), WS10M (11.9%)
   - Data leakage prevention: shift(1) on rolling features, 70/15/15 temporal split
   
2. **Multi-Objective Scheduler** - Balances three objectives
   - QoS weight: 0.4 (latency)
   - Energy weight: 0.3 (efficiency)
   - Renewable weight: 0.3 (carbon reduction)
   
3. **DVFS Controller** - 5 frequency levels (0.6-3.5 GHz) based on renewable availability

4. **INT8 Dynamic Quantization** - 4× model size reduction via PyTorch

5. **Proof-of-Stake Blockchain** - 0.001 kWh/transaction for immutable carbon credit verification

## EXPERIMENTAL RESULTS (ACTIVE - From Latest Runs)

### Baseline Comparison (10 runs × 5,000 tasks = 50,000 tasks per method)

| Method | Energy (kWh) | Carbon (gCO2) | Renewable (%) | Latency (s) |
|--------|--------------|---------------|---------------|-------------|
| Standard | 0.0655 | 18.22 | 30.47% | 2.01s |
| Compression Only | 0.0434 (-33.69%) | 12.24 (-32.81%) | 29.55% ⬇️ | 1.67s (-17%) |
| Energy Aware Only | 0.0441 (-32.70%) | 12.04 (-33.90%) | 31.70% | 1.78s (-11.76%) |
| Blockchain Only | 0.0434 (-33.69%) | 12.24 (-32.81%) | 29.55% | 1.67s (-17%) |
| EcoChain-ML (Full) | 0.0441 (-32.70%) | 12.04 (-33.90%) | 31.70% ⬆️ | 1.78s (-11.76%) |

**Key Finding:** Compression Only and Blockchain Only have IDENTICAL energy/carbon results because both use BaselineScheduler (round-robin). Energy Aware Only and EcoChain-ML have IDENTICAL energy/carbon results because both use EnergyAwareScheduler. The blockchain component adds VERIFICATION capability, not efficiency gains.
